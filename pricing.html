Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier? +

Yes. You can change your tier anytime. If you upgrade, you'll get immediate access to new chambers. If you downgrade, you'll retain access to chambers you've already unlocked.

What if I want to cancel? +

You can cancel anytime, no questions asked. Your progress is saved forever — if you return later, you'll pick up exactly where you left off.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes. The Awakening tier is free forever. You get full access to Chambers 1-3 and can experience the complete RootWise system before committing to a paid tier.

Can I get a refund? +

If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll refund your subscription in full. No questions asked.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency. Your payment is processed securely through Stripe.
